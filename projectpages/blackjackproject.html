Game in MATLAB:Ace of Cards

An Interactive Blackjack Experience

In the blackjack project, I led a team in building three games using MATLAB. My focus was on designing and implementing the blackjack game, ensuring an engaging and realistic experience for players.

Additionally, I took on the responsibility of testing not only the blackjack game but also the other games developed by the team. This involved conducting thorough testing to identify and address any bugs or issues, ensuring the games' functionality and user experience were of the highest quality.

Furthermore, I played a crucial role in task delegation, progress management, and team leadership. By effectively assigning tasks and overseeing the team's progress, I ensured the project stayed on track and met the desired objectives. This experience highlighted my ability to manage and lead a team in a collaborative software development environment.
